Beautiful on all devices

Smart switch, also known as the magic switch, is a device which provides remote access to household electronics with the use of smart phones. Magic Switch creates a flexible system such as controlling individual rooms socket and switch from anywhere using a smart phone.

  • Energy Saving
  • Easy installation
  • Carries up to 16Amps
  • Remote control from Smartphone
  • Can fit most type of wiring, no modifications required
