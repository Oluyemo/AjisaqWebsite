About Us

AJISAQ LIMITED is a proudly Nigerian high-tech company, headquarter in Yola Adamawa state
that is into manufacture of electronic devices, providing security solutions using IT,
rendering automation and ICT services to the corporate, individual and government.

MISSION

Automating individual, corporate, and government experience, process and difficulties through innovative hardware, software and services for more productivity and efficiency.

VISSION

Our intention is to build the future with the upcoming technologies of everything, we do not pursue size or power, we dream of providing solutions with hundreds and thousands of our products and services..

GOALS

The main focus of this company is on manufacturing high-quality product, excellent services provision and providing an affordable professional training programs.
